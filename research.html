Dissertation Research

Chapter 1 — Local Adaptation in Rosy-Finches

This chapter investigates local adaptation in Gray-crowned Rosy-Finches by combining whole-genome sequencing with environmental data to identify genetic variation associated with alpine climate conditions.

Chapter 2 — Historical Genomic Change in Brown-capped Rosy-Finch

Using historical museum specimens alongside contemporary genomes, this chapter examines how genetic diversity and adaptive variation have changed through time in Brown-capped Rosy-Finches.

Chapter 3 — Conservation Units and Population Decline in Bank Swallows

This chapter identifies evolutionary conservation units in Bank Swallows and evaluates how environmental change and genomic vulnerability relate to population declines across North America.
